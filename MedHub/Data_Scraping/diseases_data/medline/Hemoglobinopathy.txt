
 Hemoglobinopathy 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Hemoglobinopathy is a group of disorders in which there is abnormal production or structure of the  hemoglobin  molecule. It is passed down through families (inherited).  This group of disorders includes  hemoglobin C disease , hemoglobin S-C disease,  sickle cell anemia , and various types of  thalassemia . References
