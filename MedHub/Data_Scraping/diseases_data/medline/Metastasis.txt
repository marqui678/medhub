
 Metastasis 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Metastasis is the movement or spreading of cancer cells from one organ or tissue to another. Cancer cells usually spread through the blood or the  lymph system . If a cancer spreads, it is said to have "metastasized." Information 
 
 Whether or not cancer cells spread to other parts of the body depends on many things, including: The type of cancer The stage of the cancer Original location of the cancer  Treatment depends on the type of cancer and where it has spread. Alternative Names
