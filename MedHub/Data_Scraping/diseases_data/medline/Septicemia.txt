
 Septicemia 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Septicemia is bacteria in the blood ( bacteremia ) that often occurs with severe infections. Also called  sepsis , septicemia is a serious, life-threatening infection that gets worse very quickly. Alternative Names 
 
 Blood poisoning; Bacteremia with sepsis References
