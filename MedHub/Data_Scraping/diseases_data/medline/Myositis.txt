
 Myositis 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Myositis is an inflammation or swelling of the muscles. It is usually caused by injury, infection, medicines, or an  autoimmune disorder . See also: Dermatomyositis Polymyositis Alternative Names
