
 Ozena 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Ozena is a disease of the nose in which the bony ridges and mucous membranes of the nose waste away. Ozena is also called atrophic rhinitis. Symptoms 
 
 Nasal crusting, discharge, and a very bad smell are other common symptoms of ozena. Ozena may develop after a long period of nasal swelling. Images
