
 Keratosis obturans 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Keratosis obturans is the buildup of extra skin cells and flakey skin in the ear canal. It may be associated with pain or mild hearing loss. It is usually treated by removing the buildup of material and then medicine is applied to the ear canal. Images
