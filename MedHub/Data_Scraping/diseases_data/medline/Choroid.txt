
 Choroid 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 The choroid is the layer of blood vessels and connective tissue between the white of the eye and  retina  (at the back of the eye). It is part of the  uvea  and supplies nutrients to the inner parts of the eye. Inflammation of the choroid is called  choroiditis . Images
