
 Tubercle

 A tubercle is a small rounded point of a bone. It also refers to a  nodule  attached to bone,  mucous membrane  (moist layer lining parts of the body), or skin. The term "tubercle" is less commonly used to refer to skin irritation resulting from a tuberculosis (TB) infection. Read More
