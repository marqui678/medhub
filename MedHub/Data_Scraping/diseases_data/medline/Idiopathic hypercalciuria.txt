
 Idiopathic hypercalciuria 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Idiopathic hypercalciuria is the presence of excess calcium in the urine without an obvious cause. This condition can lead to  kidney stones  or  osteoporosis . Alternative Names
