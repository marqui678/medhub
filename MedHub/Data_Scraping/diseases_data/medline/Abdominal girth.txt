
 Abdominal girth

 Abdominal girth is the measurement of the distance around the abdomen at a specific point. Measurement is most often made at the level of the belly button (navel). Abdominal girth is used to diagnose and monitor the following: Buildup of fluid in the abdomen, most often caused by liver failure or heart failure Obesity Buildup of intestinal gas, most often caused by blockage or  obstruction in the intestines Images
