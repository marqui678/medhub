
 Neuroretinitis 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Neuroretinitis is inflammation of the  retina  and optic nerve of the eye. The condition can be caused by bacteria, viruses or autoimmune disease. It shares some features of optic neuritis. There is mostly central visual loss, which often recovers after some months, but often not completely. There is no treatment that has proven to be helpful. Images
