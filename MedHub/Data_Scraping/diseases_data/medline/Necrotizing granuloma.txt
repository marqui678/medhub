
 Necrotizing granuloma 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 A granuloma is a clump of cells that forms when the immune system tries to fight off a harmful substance but cannot remove it from the body. A necrotizing granuloma is an area of inflammation in which tissue has died. Necrotizing means dying or decaying. Tuberculosis and Wegener granulomatosis are conditions that cause necrotizing granulomas. Alternative Names
