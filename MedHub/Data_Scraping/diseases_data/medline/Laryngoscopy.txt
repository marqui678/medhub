
 Laryngoscopy 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Laryngoscopy is an exam of the voice box (larynx). It can be done using a small mirror held just below the back of your palate, or a rigid or flexible viewing tube called a laryngoscope placed in your mouth. In some cases, you may need anesthesia. Images
