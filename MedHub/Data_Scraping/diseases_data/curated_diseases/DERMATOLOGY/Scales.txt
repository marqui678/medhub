
 Scales

 Scales are a visible peeling or flaking of outer skin layers. These layers are called the stratum corneum. Causes 
 
 Scales may be caused by dry skin, certain inflammatory skin conditions, or infections.  Eczema ,  ringworm , and  psoriasis  are examples of skin disorders with scales. Alternative Names
