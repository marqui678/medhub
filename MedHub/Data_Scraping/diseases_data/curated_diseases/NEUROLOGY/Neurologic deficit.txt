
 Neurologic deficit

 A neurologic deficit refers to abnormal function of a body area due to weaker function of the brain, spinal cord, muscles, or nerves.  Examples include: Abnormal reflexes Inability to speak Decreased sensation Loss of balance Mental function problems Vision changes Walking problems Weakness  of the arms or legs  Images
