 All adults should visit their health care provider from time to time, even if they are healthy. The purpose of these visits is to: Screen for diseases Assess risk of future medical problems Encourage a healthy lifestyle Update vaccinations Maintain a relationship with a provider in case of an illness  For recommendations for men and women by age group, see: Health screening -- women -- age 18 to 39 Health screening -- women -- age 40 to 64 Health screening -- women -- over 65 Health screening -- men -- age 18 to 39 Health screening -- men -- age 40 to 64 Health screening -- men -- over 65 Alternative Names
