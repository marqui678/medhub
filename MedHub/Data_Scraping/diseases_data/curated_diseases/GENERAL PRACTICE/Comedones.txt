
 Comedones 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Comedones are small, flesh-colored, white, or dark bumps that give skin a rough texture. The bumps are caused by  acne . They are found at the opening of skin pores. A solid core can often be seen in the middle of the small bump.  Alternative Names
