
 Pedicle 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 A pedicle is a stem or stalk of tissue that connects parts of the body to each other. The brain has many pedicles (such as the cerebral pedicle) that connect areas of the brain to each other. Skin tags  are small pieces of skin tissue that connect to the body with a pedicle. Alternative Names
