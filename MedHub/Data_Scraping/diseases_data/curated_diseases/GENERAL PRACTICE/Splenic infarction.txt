
 Splenic infarction

 Splenic infarction is the death of tissue (necrosis) in the spleen due to a blockage in blood flow. Causes 
 
 Common causes of splenic infarction include: Blood clots Blood diseases such as  sickle cell anemia Infections such as  endocarditis Images
