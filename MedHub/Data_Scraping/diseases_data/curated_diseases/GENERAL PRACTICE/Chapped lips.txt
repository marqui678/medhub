
 Chapped lips

 Here are some recommendations for preventing chapped lips: Avoid excessive sun exposure Avoid going out in dry, cold weather without putting on lip balm or lipstick Try to keep the air in your home humid Use a sunscreen on your lips when outside in sunny weather  Here are some recommendations for soothing chapped and sore lips: Drink additional fluids in winter months Treat chapped lips with  beeswax  and petrolatum (Vaseline)  See your health care provider if you have sores that will not heal on your lips.
