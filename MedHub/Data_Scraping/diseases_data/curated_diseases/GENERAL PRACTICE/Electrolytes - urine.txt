
 Electrolytes - urine

 The electrolytes - urine test measures specific chemicals called  electrolytes  in urine. It usually measures the levels of calcium, chloride, potassium, or sodium. See also: Calcium - urine Chloride - urine Potassium - urine Sodium - urine Images
