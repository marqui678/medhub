
 CSD skin test

 The  cat scratch disease  (CSD) skin test was once used to help diagnose CSD. The test is no longer used today. There are better methods available to diagnose CSD, such as antibody detection by the EIA test or bacteria detection by a PCR (polymerase chain reaction) test. Images
