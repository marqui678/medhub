
 Leukemoid reaction 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 A leukemoid reaction is an increase in the white blood cell count, which can mimic  leukemia . The reaction is actually due to an infection or another disease and is not a sign of cancer. Blood counts often return to normal when the underlying condition is treated. Images
