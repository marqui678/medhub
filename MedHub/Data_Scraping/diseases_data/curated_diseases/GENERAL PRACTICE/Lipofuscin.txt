
 Lipofuscin 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Lipofuscin is a brownish pigment left over from the breakdown and absorption of damaged blood cells. Lipofuscin is found in heart muscle and smooth muscles and is also called the aging pigment. Images
