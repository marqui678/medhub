
 Pustules

 Pustules are small, inflamed, pus-filled, blister-like sores (lesions) on the skin surface. Considerations 
 
 Pustules are common in  acne . They may occur anywhere on the body, but are most commonly seen in these areas: Back Face Over the breastbone Shoulders Sweaty areas, such as the groin or armpit  Pustules may be a sign of a bacterial infection. They should be checked by a health care provider. Images
