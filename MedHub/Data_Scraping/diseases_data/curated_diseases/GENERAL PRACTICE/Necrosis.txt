
 Necrosis 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Necrosis is the death of body tissue. It occurs when too little blood flows to the tissue. This can be from injury, radiation, or chemicals. Necrosis cannot be reversed. When large areas of tissue die due to a lack of blood supply, the condition is called  gangrene . Images
