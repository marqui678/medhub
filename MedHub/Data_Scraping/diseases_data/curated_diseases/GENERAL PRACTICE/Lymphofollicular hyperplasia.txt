
 Lymphofollicular hyperplasia 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Lymphofollicular hyperplasia is an increase in the size of the lymph node follicles. These follicles contain normal white blood cells called lymphocytes. Your body produces more lymphocytes to help prevent bacteria, viruses, and other types of germs from entering the bloodstream. Alternative Names 
 
 Lymphadenopathy; Lymphofollicular hypertrophy  Images
