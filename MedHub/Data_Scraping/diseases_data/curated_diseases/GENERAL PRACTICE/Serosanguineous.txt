
 Serosanguineous 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Serosanguineous means containing or relating to both blood and the liquid part of blood (serum). It usually refers to fluids collected from or leaving the body. For example, fluid leaving a wound that is serosanguineous is yellowish with small amounts of blood. Alternative Names
