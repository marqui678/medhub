
 Incidence 
 
 
 
 To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript. 
 Incidence is the number of new cases of a condition, symptom, death, or injury that develop during a specific time period, such as a year. Incidence shows the likelihood that a person in a certain population will be affected by that condition. References
